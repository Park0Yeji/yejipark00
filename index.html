Hello! this is Yeji Park

who loves to design interactive system, especially in the Virtual Reality. Now I am studying the Gaze-Hand combined multi-modal interactions for Head-Mounted Displays. I am double majoring in Electrical Engineering and Design at UNIST. And I am working in the Wearable Interactive Technology Lab at KAIST supervised by professor Ian Oakley as undergraduate research intern to study broad about Human Centered Interaction within technical and theoretical perspectives. If you are interested in my experience, then you can check my CV and Works on this Web. Enjoy your time!
